Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about orders, shipping, and more

  1. Home
  2. FAQs

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your shipping details and payment information to complete your order. You'll receive a confirmation email once your order is placed.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, our warehouse begins processing, and changes may not be possible. Please contact our support team immediately if you need to make changes.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. Tracking updates in real-time.

What if I received a wrong or damaged item?

We're sorry about that! Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order with photos of the item. We'll arrange a free return and send you the correct item or a full refund — whichever you prefer.

How much does shipping cost?

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $150. For orders under $150, a flat rate of $9.95 applies for domestic shipping. International shipping rates vary by destination — calculated at checkout.

How long does delivery take?

Standard shipping: 3-5 business days. Express shipping: 1-2 business days. International shipping: 7-14 business days depending on location. Processing time is 1-2 business days before shipment.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customs duties and import taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with tags attached. Simply initiate a return from your account or contact our support team.

How do I initiate a return?

Log into your account, go to "My Orders," select the order, and click "Return Item." You'll receive a prepaid shipping label via email. Pack the item securely and drop it off at any authorized shipping location.

When will I receive my refund?

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Please allow 5-10 additional days for the refund to appear on your statement.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay, and Cash on Delivery (select locations). All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and are PCI DSS compliant. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through our trusted payment partners.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! LUXE gift cards are available in denominations of $25, $50, $100, $200, and $500. They can be purchased online and sent directly to the recipient via email. Gift cards never expire.

How do I create an account?

Click the user icon in the navbar or visit our registration page. You can create an account using your email or sign up instantly with Google. New members get 15% off their first order!

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a password reset link. The link expires in 24 hours for security purposes. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can request account deletion by contacting our support team at support@luxe.com. Please note that this action is permanent and all your order history, wishlist, and saved information will be deleted.

Still have questions?

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is here to help.
